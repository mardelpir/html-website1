Inception

"Inception" is a mind-bending science fiction heist film directed by Christopher Nolan, released in 2010. The movie follows the story of Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio), a skilled thief specialized in a unique form of corporate espionage called "extraction." In this world, technology allows individuals to enter and manipulate the dreams of others. Cobb's exceptional ability is to infiltrate someone's subconscious and extract valuable information while they are in a dream state.
